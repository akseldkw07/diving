# Bunaken National Park

## Overview
Indonesia's most famous wall diving. Bunaken's reefs plunge vertically from the shallows to depths of 2,000m+, creating dramatic walls encrusted with sponges, soft corals, and sea fans. Part of the Coral Triangle, the park covers five islands near Manado in North Sulawesi. Classic, accessible, and consistently excellent.

## Dates
- **Window:** Flexible — dry season (May–Oct) is ideal
- **Season:** Peak shoulder — start of dry season. Excellent conditions without peak-season (Jul–Aug) crowds.
- **Weather:** Dry season beginning. Minimal rain, calm seas.

## Diving

### Conditions
| Factor | Details |
|--------|---------|
| Visibility | 15–30m+ (excellent) |
| Water temp | 28°C (82°F) |
| Currents | Generally manageable; park is somewhat sheltered |
| Wetsuit | 3mm |

### Seasonal Events (May–June)
- Shark activity increases in June
- Green sea turtles and hawksbill turtles at cleaning stations
- Eagle ray sightings on outer walls
- No specific spawning or migration events — the walls and reef are the constant attraction

### Key Dive Sites
| Site | Depth | Highlights | Difficulty |
|------|-------|------------|------------|
| Lekuan I, II, III | 5–40m+ | The famous wall — enormous sponges, soft corals, pyramid butterflyfish, turtles, reef sharks | Moderate |
| Lekuan Pygmy | 5–25m | Wall section known for pygmy seahorses on gorgonians | Moderate |
| Tanjung Kopi (Manado Tua) | 10–30m | Two walls meeting, flat plateau, best soft coral gardens in the park | Moderate |
| Fukui Point | 5–30m | Sandy slope + wall, Napoleon wrasse, garden eels | Easy–Moderate |
| Sachiko's Point | 10–35m | Wall dive, black coral, barracuda schools | Moderate |

### Dive Plan
- 5–7 days, resort-based on Bunaken or Siladen island
- 2–3 dives/day along different wall sections
- Wide-angle lens is the play here — walls, turtles, big sponges
- Nitrox useful for extended wall profiles

## Logistics

### Getting There
- Fly to **Manado (Sam Ratulangi Airport, MDC)** — direct flights from Bali, Jakarta, Singapore
- Boat transfer from Manado mainland to Bunaken Island (~30–60 min, arranged by resort)

### Getting Out
- Boat back to Manado mainland → fly to next destination
- **Combo opportunity:** Bunaken + Lembeh is the classic North Sulawesi pairing. Same Manado airport, ~60–90 min drive apart. Some operators (Murex, Eco Divers) offer combined packages ("Passport to Paradise").

### Accommodation
- **Island resorts:** Bunaken Oasis (luxury), Bastianos (budget–mid), Two Fish, Siladen Resort
- Range: $50–300/night depending on resort tier
- Most operate as dive-and-stay packages (accommodation + diving + meals)

### Costs
| Item | Estimate (USD) |
|------|---------------|
| Resort + diving package (7 nights) | $800–3,500 |
| Domestic flight Bali → Manado | $80–200 |
| Bunaken Marine Park fee | $6/day or $17/year |
| Boat transfers | Usually included in resort package |

### Practical Info
- **Visa:** Indonesia e-VoA
- **Currency:** IDR. ATMs in Manado. Bring cash to the island — no ATMs.
- **Connectivity:** Resort WiFi available (free at many). Limited cell coverage on islands. Manado has good 4G.
- **Hyperbaric chamber:** Multiple reliable facilities in Manado — Malalayang Hospital (multiplace chamber since 1995), Manado Regional Hospital, Siloam Hospital. ~35–60 min from Bunaken Island.

## Notes
- Pairs perfectly with Lembeh Strait — wall diving + muck diving in one trip, same airport
- The Lekuan wall is genuinely iconic — one of the best wall dives in the world
- More accessible and developed than most other locations on this list
- Good value compared to Raja Ampat or Wakatobi
- Park fees are cheap — support the marine reserve
- Manado has decent restaurants and city amenities for pre/post dive days
