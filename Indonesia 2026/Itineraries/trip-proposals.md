## Flight Logistics

**SOQ → LBJ** (all combos need this transfer):
No direct flights. Route via Makassar (UPG) or Bali (DPS). Total travel: 8–12 hours including layover. Budget a full day.

**⚠️ Eid al-Adha — May 28, 2026:** Domestic flight prices surge. Book the SOQ→LBJ leg early, even before confirming liveaboards. Consider refundable tickets.

**Estimated domestic flight costs (one-way):**
- International → SOQ (via Jakarta/Bali): $150–400
- SOQ → LBJ (via UPG or DPS): $150–300
- LBJ → international (via Bali): $60–150
