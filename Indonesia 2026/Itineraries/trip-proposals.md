The early-bird powerhouse. 9N wide-coverage Raja + 6N Komodo — the longest Komodo leg available. Requires arriving May 13.

**Pros:** 15 boat nights for $7,347 — same boat nights as Aggressor+Aliikai (#2) but $900 cheaper with a longer Komodo leg (6N vs 5N). EH's "Best of Raja Ampat" covers Dampier, Wayag, Penemu, Fam. Jaya at 6N is the longest Komodo option available. 4 buffer days at end.
**Cons:** ⚠️ **Same tight transfer as combo #8.** EH ends May 22 in SOQ, Jaya boards May 23 in LBJ — need SOQ→LBJ in under 24 hours with no direct flight. Requires arriving 3 days early (May 13). EH at full price ($5,387 vs the May 24 departure's −30% discount).
**Vibe:** Early arrival pays off — if the transfer works, this is arguably the best overall combo.
